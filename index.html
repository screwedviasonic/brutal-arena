HOW TO PLAY GUIDE

Brutal Arena is an idle fighting game. You raise a comic-book brute, gear it up, and climb every ladder in the building. Fights play out on their own — your job is to build the strongest brute and choose which ladder to push.

THE CORE LOOP
  1. Fight in the Arena or Gauntlet to earn XP, gold, and loot.
  2. Spend gold & dust in the Forge and Shop to craft and upgrade gear.
  3. Equip your best loadout, level up, and push into tougher opponents.
  4. Out of stamina? Bank idle XP, spar for Focus, or climb the no-stamina Gauntlet.
TOP BAR
LV / PWR
Your brute's level and overall power rating (drives matchmaking).
Gold
Earned from fights. Spend it in the Shop and Forge.
Dust
From loot and scrapping gear. Fuels crafting and merging at the Forge.
Stamina
Each Arena fight costs 1 and regenerates over time. Gauntlet and Sparring cost none.
Legacy
Prestige currency from Ascension. Buys permanent, account-wide perks.
Name chip
Your leaderboard handle — click to rename (also renames your brute).
FIGHT
Arena
Ranked ladder. Win to gain ARP and climb divisions; lose and you drop ARP. Opponents scale with your division. Costs stamina.
Gauntlet
Endless tower, no stamina — power is the only gate. Win to climb; fall and you drop to your last boss checkpoint. Bosses and milestones pay bonus rewards.
PvP
Fight other players' defense brutes for rating. Beating someone captures them as a prisoner; losing an attack gets you jailed by the winner.
Sparring
No-stakes practice fight. Builds Focus, which multiplies your idle XP rate. Gives no direct XP — a thing to do when out of stamina.
BRUTE
Brute
Your fighter's sheet: equipped loadout, stats, records, and standings. Reroll appearance here.
Forge
Craft and upgrade weapons, pets, and skills with gold & dust. Auto-merge duplicates, auto-equip max power, manage your full inventory.
Collection
Everything you've ever owned, plus masteries — permanent bonuses for using fists, weapon types, pet species, and skill categories.
SOCIAL
Players
Search players, browse fighters near your power, add rivals, inspect their full sheet, and challenge them.
Prison
Prisoners you capture give a battle-XP buff (bigger if they out-power you). Captors holding you sap XP — break free by beating them or paying a bribe.
Battles
Your recent PvP history: who fought you, attacker or defender, win/loss, and rating change.
Tournament
Weekly event. Lock a build before Sunday 20:00 UTC; a round-robin runs and most wins takes the crown. Placement pays gold & legacy — claim from last week's results.
PROGRESS
Bounties
Rotating objectives that pay bonus rewards. You're alerted when one completes.
Shop
Rotating gear for gold, plus division-scaled stamina buffs. Refreshes on a timer, or reroll for gold.
Ascension
Endgame prestige. Hit deep Gauntlet-floor milestones for Legacy and permanent +power per tier — nothing gets wiped.
Achievements
Long-term goals tracking your milestones across every system.
GOOD TO KNOW
  • Rarity runs Common → Mythic. Higher rarity means stronger gear and a brighter aura in battle.
  • Idle XP banks while you're away (up to a cap) — claim it when you return. Prisoners and Sparring Focus boost the rate.
  • Power only counts your equipped gear, so always wear your best loadout.
  • Every level auto-grants one random stat; gear comes from level-up choices and loot drops.
  • RESET (top right) wipes everything — only use it to start completely fresh.
